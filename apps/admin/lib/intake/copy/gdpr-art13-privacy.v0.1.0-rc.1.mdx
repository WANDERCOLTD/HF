# Privacy Notice — Enrolment

This notice explains what personal data we collect when you enrol in a course on HumanFirst Foundation, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and what rights you have over it. It is provided under Article 13 of the UK GDPR and the EU General Data Protection Regulation.

## Who we are

HumanFirst Foundation is the data controller for the personal data described in this notice. You can contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@humanfirstfoundation.com.

## What we collect and why

When you enrol in a course we collect the following information about you:

- Your name and email address, so we can identify your account and contact you about the course.
- Optionally, your timezone, preferred contact method, and an accessibility note — these help us tailor the course experience to you.
- Optionally, an age range — this helps us understand our learner community in aggregate.
- A record of your consent to the platform Terms of Service and the Acceptable Use of our AI Tutor.

We process this information on the legal basis of contract under Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR — it is necessary for us to provide you with the course you are enrolling in. Marketing communications, where you opt in separately, are processed on the basis of consent under Article 6(1)(a).

## How long we keep it

We keep your enrolment record for seven years after you complete the course or your account closes, whichever is later. We keep audit-trail events that record what was disclosed and consented to for ten years. We keep special-category data (such as voluntary accessibility notes) for no longer than three years.

## Who else sees your data

Your information is hosted on infrastructure provided by Google Cloud (region: europe-west2). Conversations with the AI tutor are processed by Anthropic, an AI service provider based in the United States. We are working to confirm appropriate safeguards for international transfers under Chapter V GDPR; in the meantime each transfer is logged.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third party for their own purposes.

## Your rights

You have the right to access a copy of your data, correct it if it is wrong, ask us to delete it, ask us to limit how we use it, object to certain uses, and receive a portable copy. You have the right to withdraw any consent you gave, at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing that already happened. You can lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority — for the UK that is the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

## How to contact us about your data

For any question about your personal data or to exercise your rights, contact dpo@humanfirstfoundation.com. We will respond within one calendar month.
