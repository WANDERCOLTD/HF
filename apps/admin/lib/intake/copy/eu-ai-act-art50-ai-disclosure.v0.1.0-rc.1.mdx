# You are interacting with an AI

The enrolment assistant on this page is an AI system. Your conversation is processed by Anthropic on our behalf so the assistant can understand your responses, ask follow-up questions, and fill in the enrolment form for you.

A person from HumanFirst Foundation reviews and signs off on every enrolment before it is finalised.

This notice is provided under Article 50(1) of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (the EU AI Act).
