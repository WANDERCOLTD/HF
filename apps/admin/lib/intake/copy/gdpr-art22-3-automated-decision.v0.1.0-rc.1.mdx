# Decisions assisted by AI

Once you are enrolled, our AI tutor adapts how it teaches you based on how you respond — for example, it may change the pacing, choose different practice questions, or revise the personalised goals it sets with you. No decision that affects your access to the course or your final outcome is made by AI alone — a person from HumanFirst Foundation reviews any decision with significant effect on you.

You can ask for the reasoning behind any AI-assisted decision and request that a person review it. Contact dpo@humanfirstfoundation.com.

This notice is provided under Article 22(3) of the UK and EU GDPR.
